1. Admin - Manage Flights

  • Viewing Flights: All flights are displayed in a table, showing details such as flight number, origin, destination, date, time, and number of seats.
  • Adding Flights: Click the "Add Flight" button to navigate to the "Add Flight" page. Fill in flight details, including origin, destination, boarding and arrival times, and number of seats, then click "Submit."
  • Dynamic Updates: Newly added flights will automatically appear in the "Manage Flights" table.

2. Admin - Manage Destinations

The "Manage Destinations" page is for administrators to handle all travel destinations. Follow these steps to use this page:

  • Viewing Destinations: A list of existing destinations is displayed, including details such as destination name, airport name, and destination code.
  • Adding Destinations: Use the form at the top of the page to add new destinations. Enter details like destination name, airport information, and a unique 3-letter destination code, then click "Submit."
  • Integration with Flights: New destinations will automatically be available in the "Add Flight" page for selection.

3. User - Book a Flight

The "Book a Flight" page allows users to browse available flights and make bookings for themselves or others. Here's how to book a flight:

  • Viewing Flights: Browse flights in a table format, showing flight details like origin, destination, and time.
  • Booking a Flight: Click the "Book" button next to your chosen flight. A form will appear, allowing you to enter passenger details (name and passport number) for each traveler.
  • Submitting the Booking: After entering all details, click "Submit Booking" to confirm. A confirmation message will appear, and your booking will be saved.

4. User - Manage Bookings

The "Manage Bookings" page lets users view all their confirmed bookings. Here's what you can do:

  • Viewing Bookings: All bookings are listed in a table, showing details like flight number, date, time, origin, destination, number of passengers, and passenger details.
  • Dynamic Updates: Any new bookings you make will appear in this table without needing a page refresh.
